Overview

purpose

The purpose of creating a website for my rafting business is to reach a wider audience that I can reach potential customers beyond my immediate geographical area.

Audience

Target audience is adventure enthusiasts seeking thriing outdoor experiences in the natural environment.
 Age: Primarily between 18 and 45 years old
Financial Situation: Middle class, with disposable income for leisure activities
Interests: Outdoor leisure, nature exploation and physical fitness.

What They Want
 They want to have an exciting but safe lifting experience.
They look for detailed information about trusted rafting packages and safety equipment.

What They expect to find on the website?
 Clear descriptions of a variety rafting packages
Price and booking information (ex.group discounts)
Detailed safety guide
High-quality photos and videos
Other customers's reviews
